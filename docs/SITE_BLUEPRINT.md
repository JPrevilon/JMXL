# One-page experience blueprint

1. **Intro** — black screen, crown/JM/XL reveal and explicit “Enter the experience” action.
2. **Hero** — muted video loop, oversized Young / Black / Entrepreneur title, album and trailer CTAs.
3. **Manifesto** — “Built by vision. Backed by work. Owned from the ground up.” with editorial photo reveals.
4. **Album reveal** — pinned cover, project story, price, credits and direct purchase.
5. **Tracklist** — approved previews and persistent audio dock.
6. **Visual world** — desktop horizontal filmstrip driven by vertical scroll; swipe rail on mobile.
7. **Merch showroom** — signature tee, premium item and album bundle.
8. **Story / EPK** — artist bio, Los Angeles identity, press assets and booking.
9. **Fan capture** — simple signup with a real incentive.
10. **Outro** — large monogram, socials, contact and back-to-top.

## Motion hierarchy
- Background media moves slowly.
- Photography and product cards move at medium speed.
- Typography and buttons move faster.
- Each scene should have one dominant motion concept.
- Crossfades and shared imagery should make section boundaries feel continuous.
