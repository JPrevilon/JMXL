# JaiMoney XL content checklist

## Required before the final build

- [ ] Confirm which of the three logo concepts is final
- [ ] Export final logo as SVG and transparent PNG
- [ ] Choose hero video loop and hero poster image
- [ ] Approve final album cover
- [ ] Approve short album description and long album story
- [ ] Add complete tracklist, features, producers and durations
- [ ] Create approved 20–30 second audio previews
- [ ] Choose four to six visual/video items
- [ ] Choose three primary merch offers and final prices
- [ ] Provide inventory, size and fulfillment details
- [ ] Approve short and long artist biography
- [ ] Provide EPK PDF and press-photo download links
- [ ] Confirm booking email
- [ ] Confirm Bandcamp/download checkout URL
- [ ] Confirm merch checkout URL
- [ ] Choose email/SMS platform
- [ ] Add analytics IDs and ad pixels
- [ ] Review accessibility and reduced-motion behavior
- [ ] Test mobile, tablet and desktop
