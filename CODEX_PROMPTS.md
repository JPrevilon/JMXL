2. `01_ASSET_INTEGRATION.md` — inventory and optimize the Google Drive assets after downloading selected files into `public/media/incoming`.

5. `04_ALBUM_AUDIO_TRACKLIST.md` — connect album purchase, track data and real previews.
6. `05_VISUALS_AND_VIDEO.md` — complete the horizontal visual rail and modal playback.
7. `06_MERCH_AND_CONVERSION.md` — connect merch products, store links and conversion UI.
8. `07_STORY_EPK_FAN_CAPTURE.md` — complete biography, EPK, booking and fan capture.
9. `08_PERFORMANCE_ACCESSIBILITY_QA.md` — final optimization and launch QA.

## Recommended working method

Paste the master prompt first, then one task prompt at a time. Review and commit after every completed stage. Do not ask Codex to perform the entire build in one giant pass unless you are prepared to review a large change set.
